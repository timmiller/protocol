The Five Products

BioFlow
Stem Cells — IV Use

Wharton's jelly-derived mesenchymal stem cells that have been extra-filtered to remove larger particles (collagen, etc.) that are safe for IV administration. This is your primary stem cell product for intravenous delivery.

IV Push
BioXsomes
Exosomes — 180 Billion

A high-concentration exosome product (180 billion exosomes). Exosomes act as messengers that signal stem cells to regenerate. They are also potent anti-inflammatories. Higher count than BioAer.

IV Intra-Articular Nebulizer
BioFlex
Stem Cells — Joint Use

Wharton's jelly stem cells formulated specifically for intra-articular (joint) injection. Not filtered for IV — contains beneficial components like collagen that serve the joint environment but should not enter the vascular system.

Joints Only
BioAer
Exosomes — Lyophilized · 50B

A lyophilized (freeze-dried, powdered) exosome product with 50 billion exosomes. Must be reconstituted before use. Longer shelf life at room temperature than frozen products. Versatile — can go IV, intra-articular, or nebulized.

IV Intra-Articular Nebulizer
BioFusion
Combination Protocol

Not a separate product — BioFusion is the standard combination of BioFlex + BioAer drawn into a single syringe for one joint injection. The BioFlex delivers cells while the BioAer adds exosome signaling in the same administration.

Reconstituting BioAer

Video Walkthrough

💡
BioAer is lyophilized (powdered), similar to how peptides are prepared. It must be reconstituted before use. The liquid it's mixed into depends on the delivery route.

  1. Remove BioAer vial from storage. It is room-temperature stable until mixed.

  2. Choose your reconstitution fluid: saline for nebulization or IV; BioFlex (thawed) for joint injection (BioFusion protocol).

  3. Draw 1 cc of fluid into a syringe. Insert needle through the rubber stopper on the BioAer vial.

  4. Introduce the fluid very slowly to avoid disrupting the powder.

  5. Gently swirl (do not shake) until fully dissolved — like dissolving chocolate powder in liquid.

  6. For nebulization: reconstitute with 3 cc saline. For IV or joint: 1 cc is sufficient.

  7. Use immediately once reconstituted. Exosomes are stable, but do not leave reconstituted product sitting.

Drawing Up Product & Needle Swap

Video Walkthrough

ℹ️
Key needle specs confirmed: Use an 18g needle to draw product from the vial, then swap to a 25g needle (1" or 1.5") to inject. Never go smaller than 25g — it can shear the cells. For neuropathy/foot injections or thin patients, a 1" needle is sufficient. All other joints use 1.5".
