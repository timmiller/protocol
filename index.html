Clinical Operations Manual

This manual covers everything your clinical team needs to administer regenerative stem cell therapy safely and effectively — from product identification through aftercare. Use the navigation above or the chapter cards below to move between sections.

ℹ️
Who this is for Licensed nurse practitioners and clinical staff operating under the BIB system. All protocols are based on Regenerative Revival's clinical standards and Precision Biologics product specifications. When in doubt, confirm with your supervising NP or Seth's team.
